Our Story

Founded in 1971 in Seattle’s historic Pike Place Market, Starbucks has always stood for more than just coffee. From our very first store, we set out to build a different kind of company — one that celebrates connection, inspires human spirit, and brings people together over a cup of coffee.

Today, with thousands of stores around the world, we remain committed to ethically sourcing and roasting the highest-quality arabica coffee. We strive to create a welcoming space for everyone — a “third place” between home and work where community, creativity, and conversation can thrive.

Whether you’re here for your morning espresso, an afternoon pick-me-up, or a quiet moment to recharge, we’re honored to be part of your day. Welcome to Starbucks — it’s nice to have you here.
